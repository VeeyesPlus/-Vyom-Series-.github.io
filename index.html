Vol. I: Suryakanta - A Sci-Fi Masterpiece

Buckle up for an intergalactic adventure! My debut novel, "Suryakanta", takes you on a journey through space and time, filled with action, suspense, and unexpected twists

Main Characters

Ravi

A determined and resourceful young man on a quest to uncover the truth about his parents' fate and embark on a journey that will test his courage and resilience.

Draven Asher

Wise, enigmatic, and powerful figure with a mysterious agenda.

Mihira

Her unwavering love and sacrifice inspire awe and admiration in all who encounter her.

Aditya

A stoic and enigmatic character, haunted by his past and driven by adeep sense of duty and sacrifice.

World-Building and Setting

An enthralling tale that intertwines a cosmic mystery with human emotions, taking readers on a thrilling journey of love, sacrifice, and redemption.With its vivid characters and captivating narrative, it explores the depths of human resilience and the enduring power of hope.

Mystery & Adventure

  • Infused with enigmatic clues, hidden treasures, and unexpected twists created by Mihira.
  • From dangerous encounters to daring escapes, Ravi embarks on aquest that tests his courage and intelligence.

Myth & Tech

  • Ancient myths and legends intertwine with advanced technology.
  • A world where past and future collide, exploring the intersections of tradition and innovation.

Love & Sacrifice

  • Depths of love, depicting the bonds between Ravi and Mihira that transcend time and space.
  • Selfless choices to protect their loved ones and fight against darkness.
  • Hope and a chance at healing as Ravi confronts his past and finds his strength to forge a brighter future.
